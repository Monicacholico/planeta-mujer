Women, Business and the Law Index

Scroll to explore gender equality scores across regions. Data: World Bank, 2024.

Global Overview

The WBL index scores 190 economies on laws affecting women's economic opportunity, from 0 (most restrictive) to 100 (full equality).

Americas

From Canada's near-perfect score to ongoing challenges in Central America, the hemisphere shows wide variation in legal gender equality.

Europe

Western and Northern Europe lead globally, but Eastern Europe and Central Asia show a more mixed picture.

Africa & Middle East

The region with the widest range — from emerging reformers to economies where women face the most legal barriers.

South & Southeast Asia

A rapidly reforming region — from India's recent legal updates to emerging economies in Southeast Asia.

East Asia & Oceania

Australia and New Zealand score among the highest globally, while East Asian economies show a wide range.

The Full Picture

Zooming back out — the global gap between the highest and lowest scoring economies remains substantial.
